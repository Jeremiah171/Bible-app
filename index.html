About This App

The Bible App is designed to help you read, search, and reflect on the Holy Scriptures. Whether you want to read a chapter, find a verse, or keep personal notes, this app is your companion for daily spiritual growth.

All scripture references are from the public domain King James Version (KJV). This app is free to use and open to everyone.

May this app bless your heart and deepen your understanding of God's Word.
